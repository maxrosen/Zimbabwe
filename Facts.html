Facts About Zimbabwe

  • Zimbabwe is believed to have been the wealthy country where King Solomon got his gold and ivory.
  • The name of the country comes from the ruins "Great Zimbabwe"
  • The adult literacy rate is 90%, one of the highest in Africa
  • A big stomach on a man is a sign of wealth to Zimbabweans
  • Traditional medicine is commonly used in Zimbabwe, healers are called "n'anga"
  • Soccer, cricket, and rugby are the most popular sports

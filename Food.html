Zimbabwean Food

Mapopo Candy
- Mapopo Candy is cooked and sugared papya, a staple crop in Zimbabwe

Sadza
- Sadza is the most popular dish in Zimbabwe, it is stiff maize meal, similar to thickened porridge. It can be served with meat, gravies, sour milk, or vegetables.

Nhedzi
- Nhedzi is a traditional Zimbabwean wild mushroom soup.
